Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious grilled cheese sandwich.

For vegetarians, you may substitute the butter with your favourite non-dairy alternative and the cheese with your choice of cheese-alternative.

Ingredients

  • your favourite bread, 2 slices
  • butter
  • cheddar cheese
  • provolone cheese
  • mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Shred the cheeses.
  3. Place buttered-sides of bread face-down on warm non-stick pan.
  4. Mix the three cheeses in one bowl to combine.
  5. Sprinkle your desired amount of cheese on both sides of bread.
  6. Allow cheese to melt - may take up to 5 minutes.
  7. Using a spatula, flip to close the sandwich.
  8. Press down with spatula and flip a couple of times.
  9. Remove from pan and cut sandwich diagonally into two triangles.
  10. Serve with ketchup and tomato soup and enjoy!
