Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious pepperoni pizza.

For vegetarians, just omit the pepperoni and replace with your favourite toppings.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • yeast
  • sugar
  • salt
  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. Combine flour, water, yeast, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. Knead dough for 15 minutes.
  3. Allow dough to rise for 2 hours.
  4. Lightly flour table and roll out dough.
  5. Roll dough into 30 cm circle - leaving an edge for crust.
  6. Spoon tomato sauce onto pizza dough.
  7. Top with cheese and slices of pepperoni.
  8. Bake in 375 degree Fahrenheit oven for 18-20 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 minutes.
  10. Slice into eighths and enjoy!
