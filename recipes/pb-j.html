Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Delicious hot or cold. Toast your bread or grill the sandwich with a bit of butter if you want a crispy version.

Ingredients

  • bread, 2 slices
  • peanut butter
  • jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter onto one side of a slice of bread.
  2. Spread jelly onto the other slice.
  3. Flip the peanut butter side onto the jelly side to combine the sandwich.
  4. Press down with gentle pressure to adhere both sides.
  5. Slice diagonally into 2 triangles.
  6. Serve with a cold glass of your choice of milk or milk-alternative beverage and enjoy!
